Process

How It Works

Three steps. That's literally it.

01

Scan QR & Send File

Open your camera, scan the QR code on the kiosk. Upload your file via the secure link — PDF, DOCX, JPG, all supported. Get your unique PIN instantly.

~10 sec
02

Enter PIN & Customise

Type your PIN at the kiosk screen. Choose copies, colour vs B&W, single or double-sided, paper size. The kiosk is ready in seconds.

~15 sec
03

Pay & Collect

Scan the UPI QR or tap your card. Your pages come out instantly. The moment printing is done — your file is permanently deleted. Zero trace.

~30 sec
