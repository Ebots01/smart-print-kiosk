How It Works

Three simple steps to secure, instant printing.

01

Message the Bot

Send your document securely to our dedicated WhatsApp or Telegram messaging bot. Your file is encrypted and sent straight to our cloud server.

02

Start Print

Walk up to the One Tap Print kiosk and enter the unique 4-digit release code provided by the bot to authenticate your session.

03

Collect & Go

Your document prints in seconds. Once completed, your file is permanently auto-deleted from the server to guarantee your privacy.
