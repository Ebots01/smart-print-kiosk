How It Works

Print in 4 Simple Steps

Fast. Secure. Completely contactless. No sign-up, no staff, no waiting.

01
📱

Scan the Kiosk QR

Each SwiftPrint kiosk has a unique QR code. Just point your phone camera — no app download needed.

02
📤

Upload Your Document

Choose any file from your phone, laptop or Google Drive. Supported: PDF, Word, Images. No account required.

03
⚙️

Set Print Preferences

Choose number of copies, Black & White or color, single or double-sided, and orientation.

04
🗂️

Collect Your Print

Enter the 4-digit OTP on the kiosk and collect your printout instantly. Your file is auto-deleted after printing.
