How It Works

Three simple steps to secure, instant printing.

01

Scan QR & Send File

Scan the QR code to open the bot. Send your document securely and receive a unique PIN code instantly.

02

Enter PIN & Customize

03

Pay & Collect

Scan the payment QR on the screen to pay instantly. Collect your printed pages as your file gets auto-deleted.
